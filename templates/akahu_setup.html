Bank Account Integration

About Akahu Integration

Akahu is a secure financial data platform that allows Rent Tracker to access your bank account transactions to automatically detect rent payments.

  • Your banking credentials are never stored by Rent Tracker
  • We only access transaction data, not your account balances or personal information
  • You can revoke access at any time through your Akahu account
Getting Your Akahu Credentials

To obtain your Akahu App Token and User Token:

  1. Visit the Akahu Developer Portal
  2. Create a developer account if you don't have one
  3. Create a new application to get your App Token
  4. Generate a User Token for your bank account
  5. Enter both tokens below
